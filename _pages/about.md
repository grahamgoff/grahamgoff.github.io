I am a Ph.D. student in the [Department of Government](https://liberalarts.utexas.edu/government/) at the University of Texas at Austin. Methodologically, I work on causal inference, causal graphs, and questions of measurement and identification. My substantive research focuses on international political economy, particularly the intersection of conflict and trade. I co-author the R package [DAGassist](https://cran.r-project.org/package=DAGassist) on CRAN.

I previously completed an M.I.A. at the [Bush School of Government and Public Service](https://bush.tamu.edu/) at Texas A&M University.

I can be contacted at goffgrahamc[at]gmail and my CV is available [here](/files/cv.pdf).
