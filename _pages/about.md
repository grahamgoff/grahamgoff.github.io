I am a Ph.D. student in the [Department of Government](https://liberalarts.utexas.edu/government/) at the University of Texas at Austin. I previously completed an M.I.A. at the [Bush School of Government and Public Service](https://bush.tamu.edu/) at Texas A&M University.

My substantive research interests lie in international political economy, with a focus on conflict and trade. Methodologically, I work on causal inference, directed acyclic graphs, and questions of measurement and identification.

I co-author the R package [DAGassist](https://cran.r-project.org/package=DAGassist) (CRAN), a toolkit for applied DAG-based causal analysis.

## Research Interests

**Substantive:** International political economy; conflict and trade

**Methodological:** Causal inference; directed acyclic graphs; measurement and identification
